What we Offer

If you want to get into outdoor rock climbing, but don't know where to start, our services are just for you! We offer you the opportunity to select one of our professional guides to take you rock climbing in the great outdoors. In addition, we have a wide variety of rock climbing equipment which you can rent in you don't have your own climbing gear. We offer you the ability to rent one of our guides with professional experience in various types of outdoor climbing such as:
  • Bouldering
    • Bouldering involves climbing short, challenging routes, called problems, without ropes but with crash pads for protection.
  • Trad-Climbing
    • Trad-climbing, or traditional climbing, requires climbers to place and remove their own protective gear in the rock as they ascend, relying on natural features for support.
  • Sport-Climbing
    • Sport-climbing is a form of rock climbing where climbers follow routes with pre-placed bolts for protection, focusing on athletic ability and technical skill.
